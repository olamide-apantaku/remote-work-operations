## Core KPIs

| KPI | Full Name | What It Measures |
|---|---|---|
| CSAT | Customer Satisfaction Score | Customer happiness after interaction |
| NPS | Net Promoter Score | Customer loyalty and advocacy |
| AHT | Average Handle Time | Efficiency of each interaction |
| FCR | First Contact Resolution | Issues resolved on first contact |
| SLA | Service Level Agreement | Response and resolution time targets |
| TTR | Time to Resolution | Total time to fully resolve an issue |
| TBR | Ticket Backlog Rate | Volume of unresolved tickets |
| ACR | Agent Compliance Rate | Adherence to processes and standards |

## KPI Improvement Strategies

**Improving CSAT:**
- Use empathy statements in every interaction
- Confirm resolution before closing every ticket
- Follow up within 24 hours on complex issues
- Personalise every interaction — use customer name
- Send CSAT survey immediately after resolution

**Improving NPS:**
- Build genuine relationships with customers
- Go above and beyond on every interaction
- Proactively update customers on issue progress
- Resolve issues faster than expected
- Ask for feedback and act on it

**Improving AHT:**
- Use templates and macros for common issues
- Invest in deep product knowledge
- Prepare for common issues before shift starts
- Avoid unnecessary back and forth
- Document interactions in real time not after

**Improving FCR:**
- Fully understand the issue before responding
- Check CRM history before every interaction
- Avoid transferring customers unnecessarily
- Escalate only when absolutely necessary
- Confirm resolution before closing ticket

**Improving SLA Compliance:**
- Review ticket queue every hour
- Prioritise tickets approaching SLA deadline
- Use triage system for high volume periods
- Escalate blockers to Team Lead immediately
- Never leave a ticket without a status update
